Our work

Minerva,The Literary Club Of ABES Engineering College is considered as one of the most prestigious clubs of the college. The Club started its journey five years ago, in the year 2013. In these four years, the Club has achieved a lot with the help of hardwork and dedication shown by the members associated with it and hence, is now one of the most reputed clubs in our college. The Club is known for it's level of sincerity, hardwork, dedication, management and knowledge possessed by its members. Apart from academical growth, personal growth is also required. The college provides an environment to inculcate professionalism in oneself. We, at the literary club will help you to furnish your talent and skills. Your association with the Club will definitely give you the essence of pride and feeling of being an elite.
